Apply for Cancer Control Grants

The Division of Cancer Control and Population Sciences funds a large portfolio of grants and contracts. The portfolio currently includes approximately 800 grants valued at nearly $450 million. Here we provide a listing of funding opportunities that are currently accepting applications. Please visit this page regularly as new funding opportunities are added upon approval by NCI.

For information on the portfolio of over 800 grants, please go the Research Portfolio section of this website.

Announcements are Available Online for the Following Areas:
  • Parent and Omnibus Funding Announcements
  • Cancer Survivorship
  • Epidemiology and Genomics Research
  • Healthcare Delivery Research
  • Health Disparities
  • Implementation Science
  • Surveillance Research
  • Basic Biobehavioral and Psychological Sciences
  • Health Behaviors Research
  • Health Communication and Informatics Research
  • Science of Research & Technology
  • Tobacco Control Research
  • FOAs with an R03 Component
  • FOAs with an R21 Component
