Research Portfolio

Currently funded and archived research portfolio for the Implementation Science funding opportunities:

  • Dissemination & Implementation Grants (R01, R03, R21)
    Grant applications that will identify, develop, and refine effective and efficient methods, structures, and strategies that test models to disseminate and implement research-tested health behavior change interventions and evidence-based prevention, early detection, diagnostic, treatment, and quality of life improvement services into public health and clinical practice settings.
  • Dissemination & Diffusion Supplements (2002-2008)
    Administrative supplements for NCI-funded r01, P01, P50, U01, and U19 grants pertinent to cancer control
