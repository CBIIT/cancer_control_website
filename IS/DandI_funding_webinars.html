NCI D&I Funding Related Webinars

In January 2013, the Trans-NIH Funding Announcements for Dissemination and Implementation Research in Health (PAR-13-055 (R01); PAR-13-054 (R21); and PAR-13-056 (R03)) were re-issued.  Two webinars were hosted as an opportunity for potential applicants interested in learning more about the re-issued announcements.  The first webinar, held January 31, 2013, was offered to all potential applicants and featured NCI’s Dir. Russell Glasgow, NIMH’s, Dr. David Chambers, and the current chair of the Dissemination and Implementation Research in Health (DIRH) study section, Dr. Ross Brownson. The second, held April 22, 2013, was specific to international applicants and focused more in depth on the unique issues and questions that international applicants face. The April session was conducted by NIMH’s Dr. David Chambers and NIH Fogarty International Center’s Dr. Joshua Rosenthal. These sessions have been recorded and archived below.
