ISC3 Annual Grantee Meeting

  • Home

  • ISC3 Annual Grantee Meeting
Annual Meeting hosted by:

The Implementation Science Centers in Cancer Control (ISC3) Annual Grantee Meeting will be held virtually on September 21, 2020.

The 2020 meeting will focus on unique and shared goals across the work groups; goal setting for year two; evaluation strategies and expectations; and strategies for investigator engagement. There will be a variety of presentations and discussion sessions, as well as pre-work prior to encourage more engagement and discussion during each virtual meeting.

Agenda

September 21, 2020
Virtual ISC3 Grantee Meeting

11:00-11:45 am ET

Laboratory & Capacity Building: Work Group Interactive Presentations
Objectives:

  • Summarize accomplishments to date of each work group
  • Discuss desire and/or need to expand work group membership
  • Discuss work group synergy and harmonization
  • Review Year 2 goals, objectives, and anticipated deliverables

Moderator: Cynthia Vinson

Lunch Break (1 hour, 15 minutes)

1:00-1:45 pm ET

Introduction of ISC3 Initiative and Critical Role of Laboratory Partners Panel
Objectives:

  • Review impact of COVID on the laboratory context
  • Discuss questions to be addressed in cancer in the new treatment environment
  • Examine how ISC3 could support implementation efforts in clinical and community-based settings (research & practical)

Moderator: TBD

Break (45 minutes)

2:30-3:15 pm ET

Cross-Center Evaluation & Measurement: Work Group Interactive Presentations
Objectives:

  • Summarize accomplishments to date of each work groups
  • Discuss desire and/or need to expand work group membership
  • Discuss work group synergy and harmonization
  • Review Year 2 goals, objectives, and anticipated deliverables

Moderator: April Oh

Break (45 minutes)

4:00-5:00 pm ET

Summary and Next Steps

Objectives:

  • Summarize key findings from work group presentations
  • Discuss next steps for cross-fertilization of work groups  
  • Discuss ISC3 initiative evaluation strategies
  • Review Social Network Data and Implications

Moderators: Kristie Foley,
April Oh, Ross Brownson
Registration

This meeting is free to attend. Please complete the registration form to note your interest in attending. Registration approval will be required by your Center’s Principal Investigator and notice of acceptance will be provided at the latest the week of September 11, 2020.
