Webinar on Funding for Cognitive Neuroscience in Behavioral Research

The National Cancer Institute (NCI) Behavioral Research Program will host a webinar on Wednesday, September 14, 11:00 a.m. to noon EST. Register today!

This webinar will provide key information about the new R01 and R21 funding opportunity announcement, Leveraging Cognitive Neuroscience Research to Improve Assessment of Cancer Treatment Related Cognitive Impairment (PAR-16-212 [R01] and PAR-16-213 [R21]).

Senior Scientist Dr. Jerry Suls of the NCI Behavioral Research Program, will provide an overview of the new funding opportunity aimed at improving the assessment of cognitive impairment following cancer treatment for non-CNS malignancies in adults, sometimes colloquially known as “chemobrain.”

Speaker:
Jerry Suls, PhD

Moderator:
Paige Green, PhD, MPH

 

Intended Audience: Extramural investigators at junior, mid-, and senior levels.
Email your grantee question in advance to ncidccpsbrpadvances@mail.nih.gov.
