Process of Care Research Branch (PCRB)

Key Initiatives

The Process of Care Research Branch has four priority research areas. The branch is currently developing science in the following areas through interactions with investigators and various projects:

  • Care Transitions
  • Health Care Teams
  • Cancer Screening
  • Shared Decision Making
