The Impact of Shared Decision Making on Outcomes in the Process of cancer Care
March 6, 2013 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM EST

Glyn Elwyn, PhD
Co-Director, 2011 Summer Institute for Informed Patient Choice
Professor of Primary Care Medicine
Research Director, Department of Primary Care and Public Health, Cardiff University
General Practitioner, North Cardiff Medical Centre
Cardiff, Wales, UK Co-Director, Microsystem Academy
Center for Leadership and Improvement
The Dartmouth Institute


Glyn Elwyn, BA, MB, BCh, FRCGP, PhD is a primary care clinician with research interests in shared decision making, risk communication, the design and evaluation of decision support interventions and the integration of health informatics into clinical practice. He was appointed inter-school Distinguished Research Chair at Cardiff University in May 2005, and is the Chair of the Clinical Epidemiology Interdisciplinary Research Group at the School of Medicine and Director of Research at the Department of Primary Care and Public Health. Together with Professor Adrian Edwards, he convenes a decision laboratory (www.decisionlaboratory.com). The lab has developed a suite of web-based interactive patient decision support (see www.prosdex.com, www.amniodex.com, www.bresdex.com), tools that have been adapted in 2011 by NHS Direct for use in the UK. He Chairs the International Patient Decision Aids Standards Collaboration and has been a member of the lead faculty at the Summer Institutes for Informed Patient Choice, Dartmouth College, New Hampshire (2007-10). He co-edits 'Shared decision making: Evidence Based Patient Choice' (Oxford University Press, 2nd edition 2009) and developed the OPTION instrument to measure shared decision making (http://www.optioninstrument.com/).
