Forms for CRTAs to Accept the Fellowship

The following information applies ONLY to accepted candidates of a Cancer Research Training Award (CRTA) Fellowship in the Behavioral Research Program. If you have not received a signed acceptance letter from the Fellowship Mentor, please do not complete these forms.

Fellowship awards cannot be activated until all forms are received. If you have questions, please contact Ms. Joan Becker at beckerj@mail.nih.gov or 301-435-6815.

Under the terms of your CRTA, you must be covered by adequate health insurance. We provide Foundation for Advanced Education in the Sciences (FAES) Insurance free of charge through Blue Cross Blue Shield. For more information, see: http://www.faes.org/health_insurance.htm . You must indicate your FAES Insurance Election (section 3 of form #4 in the checklist below) or decline and provide proof of private insurance coverage.

Checklist of required forms to accept your award:

  1. Self-Identification of Handicap (SF 256)
  2. Ethnicity and Race Identification (SF 181)
  3. Provisions Agreement (PDF)
  4. Insurance Application (PDF)
    If you choose to decline insurance through your Fellowship, send proof of private insurance (Photocopy of Insurance ID Card).
  5. FAES Eligibility Information Signature Form (DOC)
  6. Automated Clearing House (ACH) Payment Enrollment (SF 3881)
    Complete the second section ONLY (Payee Information); provide a signature in the Contact Field, and attach a Voided Personal Check.
  7. Approved Training Plan
    You should have received a training plan enclosed in your acceptance letter from the Fellowship Mentor. Please review and sign the plan; details of your training will be discussed with your Mentor when you arrive for your Fellowship.
  8. Proof of Citizenship
    Photocopy of Birth Certificate or Passport
  9. Official Transcripts and/or Proof of Academic Good Standing
    Send transcript of highest Degree conferred. If currently enrolled in school, Proof of Academic Good Standing must be sent on official letterhead and signed by graduate program director, advisor, or equivalent.

Please review all forms carefully; sign and date, and mail to:

Ms. Joan A. Harris
Behavioral Research Program
6130 Executive Blvd.
Rockville, MD 20892-7326

Once we receive all required information, you will be entered into our NIH Employee Database (NED). At that point you will receive an email from NED and will be issued an NIH Identification Number. You should then complete the two mandatory online computer security trainings (http://irtsectraining.nih.gov/).

  • Information Security Awareness
  • Privacy Awareness Course

You will need your NIH ID# for this. It is best to complete the trainings before you arrive for your Fellowship.
