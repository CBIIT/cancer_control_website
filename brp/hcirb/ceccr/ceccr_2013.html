The Centers of Excellence in Cancer Communication Research (CECCR) 2013 Grantee Meeting will be held on August 19-21 in Ann Arbor Michigan. Participants will engage with the scientific community in a moderated review of the communication research findings uncovered over the past ten years.

 

DATE

August 19 – 21, 2013

LOCATION

University of Michigan North Campus Research Complex
Building 10 Research Auditorium
2800 Plymouth Road, Ann Arbor, MI 48109

SCHEDULE

The meeting will begin on Monday, August 19, 2013, at 9:00 am.
The meeting will conclude on Wednesday, August 21, 2013 at 1:00pm.
