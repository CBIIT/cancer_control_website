NIH Award Process

This NIH Office of Extramural Research web page includes an overview of the award process with specific details of the pre- and post-award process.

eRA Commons

eRA systems provide applicants, grantees and federal staff the tools necessary for electronic processing of grants.

Non-Competing Continuation Progress Report

Link to downloadable instructions and forms for Non-Competing Continuation Progress Reports (PHS 2590).

Updated Non-Competing Continuation Progress Report Instructions

Notice published in NIH Guide to Grants and Contracts to clarify instructions for completing Non-Competing Continuation Progress Reports.

Clarification of All Personnel Report for Non-Competing Continuation Progress Reports

Notice published in NIH Guide to Grants and Contracts clarifying the information to be reported on the All Personnel Report.

No cost extensions

Information from NIH Grants Policy Statement summarizing extension of final budget period of a previously approved project period without additional NIH funds.

NIH Public Access Policy

Information about NIH requirement for investigators to submit manuscripts to PubMed Central upon acceptance for publication.

Prior Approval Requirements

Section of NIH Grants Policy Statement summarizing actions requiring NIH prior approval.

Change in Grantee Institution

Section of NIH Grants Policy Statement describing process of transferring a grant from one institution to another.

Modification of Aims and Approaches

Details about EGRP's requirement for investigators to seek approval for modification of specific aims, methodology or approach.
