Small Grants Program for Behavioral Research in Cancer Control Annual Grantee Meeting

The Small Grants Program for Behavioral Research in Cancer Control Annual Grantee Meeting will be held on January 12 -13, 2009 in EPN Conference Room C-F. Our goal for this annual meeting is to provide participants who are near the completion of their current small grant funding with strategic guidance and mentioning on the next phase of their research support, and to foster research collaboration and networking.
