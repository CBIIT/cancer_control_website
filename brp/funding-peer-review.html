NIH Peer Review Process

This section contains links to help investigators learn more about tools available to manage grants after the submission of applications.

  • What Happens to Your Grant Application: A Primer for New Applicants
    Brief description of the peer review process for new applicants from the NIH Center for Scientific Review (CSR).
  • NIH Peer Review Process
    Webpage developed by NIH's Office of Extramural Research (OER) that provides an overview of the peer review process, along with details of first- and second-level review, and post-review advice for funded and unfunded grants.
  • Tracking Submitted Applications
    NIH Webpage with information on how to track submitted applications during various phases of the grants process.
  • NIH Enhancements to Peer Review
    NIH Webpage describing enhancements to the peer review process that began in 2008 and are ongoing.
  • Study Section Rosters
    This page developed by the Center for Scientific Review includes links to the various NIH study sections, descriptions of their focus, and membership rosters.
  • Appeals of Initial Scientific Review
    Link to the NIH policy for appeal procedures related to the peer review process.
