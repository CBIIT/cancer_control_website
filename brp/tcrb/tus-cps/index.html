The Tobacco Use Supplement to the Current Population Survey

  • Home
  • Program Branches
  • TCRB
  • TUS-CPS

The Tobacco Use Supplement to the Current Population Survey (TUS-CPS) is an NCI-sponsored survey of tobacco use that has been administered as part of the U.S. Census Bureau's Current Population Survey approximately every 3-4 years since 1992-93. The most recent publicly released data are from the 2014-2015 TUS-CPS wave, co-sponsored with the Food and Drug Administration’s Center for Tobacco Products (FDA-CTP).
