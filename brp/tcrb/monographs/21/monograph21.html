How to search within the PDF:

  1. Open document
  2. Press CTRL + F at the same time
  3. A search box will appear on the right side of your browser window
  4. Type what you are looking for
  5. Results will show up in the bottom part of the search box
  6. Click on what best fits your search
  7. If there is nothing that suits your search, try redefining
