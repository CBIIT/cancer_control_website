Tobacco Control Research Branch (TCRB)

NCI Tobacco Control Monograph Series

 

The Tobacco Control Research Branch (TCRB) leads and collaborates on research, and disseminates evidence-based findings to prevent, treat, and control tobacco use. TCRB is within the National Cancer Institute’s (NCI) Behavioral Research Program (BRP), in the Division of Cancer Control and Population Sciences (DCCPS).
