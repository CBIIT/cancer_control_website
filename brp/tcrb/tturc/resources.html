Pre-Application Meeting

Applicants are strongly encouraged to contact program staff with any unanswered questions. This page will be updated as new questions arise

The electronic TTURC RFA Pre-Application Meeting that was held on Tuesday, November 18 can be viewed via NIH Video Casting. This requires RealPlayer software and 220Kbps LAN or 56Kbps dial-up bandwidth. Viewers outside the NIH network can download the latest free version of RealPlayer software from Real Networks (http://www.real.com/). To access the Webcast, go to http://videocast.nih.gov. The videocast is currently listed under: past events, 50 most recent videocasts, Transdisciplinary Tobacco Research Centers Pre-Application Meeting.
