Current Research

The Active Research Grant Portfolio displays a list of grants/contracts within the OCS Portfolio with abstracts, active as of January. 2002. The list will be updated three times a year according to the NCI’s National Cancer Advisory Board Meetings (January, May and September).

Chart 1 shows the distribution of the OCS research portfolio by funding mechanism: R01s, R03s, R21s, P30s, R13s, Minority Supplements and a U01 and a P50.

Chart 2 shows the distribution of the OCS research portfolio by grant focus: physiologic, psychosocial or health behavior interventions, dual focus on physiologic and psychosocial late effects, psychosocial late effects only (i.e. depression, resilience) physiologic late effects only (i.e. Lymphedema, cognitive functioning), economic late effects, outcomes research/patterns and quality of care, and health behaviors of survivors.
