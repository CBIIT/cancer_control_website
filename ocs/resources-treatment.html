Cancer Treatment and Late-term Effects

http://www.cancer.gov/cancertopics/coping/
These PDQ's (Physician Data Query) provide descriptions of the pathophysiology and treatment of common physical and psychosocial complications of cancer and its treatment. Also includes PDQ's on nutritional and emotional concerns, late-term effects, survivorship and end-of-life issues.
