Research

The current OCS portfolio consists of 15 RO1’s, 6 RO3’s, 1 P50, 20 supplements to P30 Cancer Center grants, Co-Operative Agreements, and Contracts, (Total N=42). Survivorship content areas addressed by the grant applications include: Physiologic (18), Psychosocial (18), Economic (3), and Surveillance (3). During FY99, the OCS has continued to fund approximately 1-2 new R01s and 2 new RO3s per review cycle (3 times a year). Additionally, a trans-NIH collaboration has led to the funding of a P50 grant application for the establishment of a Center for the Study of the Mind Body Interactions and Health. Please link to the NCI Division of Extramural Activity at http://deainfo.nci.nih.gov/funding.htm, for a description of these funding opportunities.
