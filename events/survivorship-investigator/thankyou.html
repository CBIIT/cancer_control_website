Thank You for Your Registration

  1. DCCPS Home
  2. Cancer Survivorship Investigator Meeting
  3. Thank You

Thank you for registering for the Cancer Survivorship Investigator Meeting.

The meeting will take place April 8, 2019, at the National Cancer Institute's Shady Grove Campus in Rockville, MD.

We will send an email with additional details and pre-readings prior to the meeting.

Hotel Arrangements

A block of rooms has been reserved at the Courtyard Gaithersburg Washingtonian Center for the night of Sunday, April 7th, at a rate of $251 plus tax. Reservations at that rate can be made by clicking here or by calling Marriott Reservations at 1(800) 321-2211 or (301) 527-9000 on or before March 17th, 2019.  Please identify yourself as part of the NIH group staying at the Courtyard Gaithersburg Washingtonian Center, located at 204 Boardwalk Place, Gaithersburg, MD  20878.  Reminder that meeting budget constraints prevent NCI from underwriting participants’ travel.

A shuttle will take hotel guests from the hotel to the meeting on Monday morning, and will return guests to the hotel at the conclusion of the meeting.

Agenda

The agenda for the meeting is available here (PDF).

Background Materials

In preparation for the meeting, we ask that participants read the following materials.

  • Alfano CM, Leach CR, Smith TG, Miller KD, Alcaraz KI, Cannady RS, Wender RC, Brawley OW. Equitably Improving Outcomes for Cancer Survivors and Supporting Caregivers: A Blueprint for Care Delivery, Research, Education, and Policy. CA: Cancer J Clin 2019;69:35-49.
  • Rowland JH, Gallicchio L, Mollica M, Saiontz N, Falisi AL, Tesauro G. Survivorship Science at the NIH: Lessons Learned From Grants Funded in Fiscal Year 2016. JNCI J Natl Cancer Inst (2019) 111(2): djy208.
  • Kline RM, Arora NK, Bradley CJ, Brauer ER, Graves DL, Lunsford NB, McCabe MS, Nasso SF, Nekhlyudov L, Rowland JH, Schear RM, Ganz PA. Long-Term Survivorship Care After Cancer Treatment - Summary of a 2017 National Cancer Policy Forum Workshop JNCI J Natl Cancer Inst (2018) 110(12): djy176.

Additional Information

If you have any questions, please contact Nicole Saiontz at saiontzn@mail.nih.gov or Taccara Briggs at briggst@mail.nih.gov.

We look forward to your participation.
