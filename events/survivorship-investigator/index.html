Evidence-Based Approaches for Survivorship Care: Where are the Research Gaps?

National Cancer Institute Shady Grove Campus
April 8, 2019

Purpose: Following the National Academies’ Long-term Survivorship Care After Cancer Treatment: Proceedings of a Workshop (2018), which reviewed progress since the 2006 IOM report From Cancer Patient to Cancer Survivors: Lost in Transition, there is a need to identify research gaps in the recommended components of survivorship care and to identify strategic research priorities.

Planning Committee:

Internal: Janet de Moor, PhD, MPH; Margaret Farrell, MPH, RD; Lisa Gallicchio, PhD; Paige Green, PhD, MPH, FABMR; Deborah Mayer, PhD, RN, AOCN, FAAN; Nicole Saiontz, MHS; and Li Zhu, PhD
External: Julia Rowland, PhD, and Catherine Alfano, PhD
Facilitator: Trish Silber
