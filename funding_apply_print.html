NCI Provocative Questions Initiative

The NCI Provocative Questions initiative looks for bold new approaches to answer perplexing scientific questions. NCI has issued 20 new Provocative Questions (PQs) for 2014 on the NCI PQ website. The questions are grouped into five themes:

  • Group A: Cancer Prevention and Risk
  • Group B: Mechanisms of Tumor Development or Recurrence
  • Group C: Tumor Detection, Diagnosis, and Prognosis
  • Group D: Cancer Therapy and Outcomes
  • Group E: Clinical Effectiveness

Researchers are invited to apply for R01 and R21 grants in these five thematic areas by responding to the NCI Provocative Questions Requests for Applications. Letters of intent are due December 16, 2013, and applications will be due January 15, 2014.

The contact for NCI Provocative Questions relevant to Cancer Control and Population Sciences is Deborah M. Winn, Ph.D. The primary NCI contact for the PQ initiative is Emily Greenspan, Ph.D.
