Research Portfolios

  1. Home
  2. Research Portfolios

DCCPS funds cancer control research to understand the causes and distribution of cancer in populations, support the development and delivery of effective interventions, and monitor and explain cancer trends. Central to these activities are evaluating what has been learned, identifying new priorities and strategies, and effectively applying research discoveries to reduce the cancer burden.
