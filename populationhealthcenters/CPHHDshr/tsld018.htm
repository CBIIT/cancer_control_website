Purpose of RFA

  • Catalyze interdisciplinary research to determine the nature and impact of social determinants on disease

  • Integration of research from multiple areas

    • Pre-disease biologic pathways
    • Environment and gene expression
    • Personal and social ties
    • Community ‘health’
    • Positive health – resilience
    • Known risk factors
    • Health care system
    • Inequalities in outcomes
    • Methodologic research

  • Support development and implementation of interventions to reduce overall burden and disparities in disease incidence and outcomes
