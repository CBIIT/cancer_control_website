Research Emphasis

DCCPS manages a portfolio of almost 900 active grants (more than $450 million), including such topics as:

  • Epidemiology & Genomics
  • Behavioral Research
  • Applied Research
  • Surveillance Research
  • Survivorship Research

The following information is not meant to be an all-inclusive list of the research interests across the division. The intent of such research emphasis is to mobilize the research community and encourage investigators to submit grant proposals that will enhance our knowledge in these core areas.
