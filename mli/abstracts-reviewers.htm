Call for Abstracts

Instructions for Poster Abstract Reviewers


Timeline of Review Process

Monday, October 18, 2010
Web site opens to begin reviewing abstracts. Note: This date may be earlier if we make the abstract submission deadline earlier.

Friday, November 12, 2010
Review Web site closes. All abstract reviews MUST be completed and saved by midnight Eastern time.

Reviewer Instructions:

  1. Access the abstract review Web site via http://cancercontrol.cancer.gov/mli/.
  2. Further details on how to read and submit abstract reviews will be inserted here once available from the contractor.
  3. The aim is to ensure that all abstracts are reviewed according to a consistent and rigorous set of standards and criteria. Please answer the questions in the Abstract Submission Guidelines document to make a best judgment about the content and quality of the submission, and to offer a summary rating. Please note that these reviews will be used to (a) catalog conference submissions and (b) provide a consistent framework for comparing abstracts for acceptance.
